⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

Known risks.
Mitigated.

Every risk identified, every mitigation mapped. No surprises.

SEO algorithm changes
Medium
Diversify from Day 1: Google Ads + LinkedIn + email newsletter. SEO is primary but never the only channel.
Regulatory (money custody)
Low – Phase 1
No fund custody in Phase 1. Pure comparison layer. Legal review mandatory before Phase 4 API infrastructure.
FX data accuracy
High
Real-time FX APIs from day one. Clear "rates may vary" disclaimers on all comparison pages. No invented prices.
AI hallucinating prices
High
AI explains only — prices always sourced from live APIs, never generated. LLM output is text-only, not data.
Trust gap (new brand)
Medium
Editorial content, SSL, privacy policy, partner logos, and transparency pages from launch. Build credibility through content.
Google Ads cost overrun
Medium
Strict daily caps from day 1. Optimise Quality Score to reduce CPC. Monitor ROAS weekly and pause underperformers.
AI API cost at scale
Medium
Aggressive result caching. At Phase 3 volume, move to own fine-tuned model. Cost ~$50–200/mo at MVP scale.
Competitive copycat
Low–Medium
Data moat + provider contracts + B2B relationships create switching costs. Speed and execution are the real barriers.
